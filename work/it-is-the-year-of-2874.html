I led the visual direction for Klankz NFT by setting a distinctive chibi illustration style that emphasized a playful and expressive identity within a dark-themed Web3 environment. I worked closely with the illustrator to define the art style, character proportions, and visual tone to ensure each asset felt cohesive, cute, and community-focused. Beyond static visuals, I also handled the animation and motion design—bringing the NFTs to life through glitch animations, token reveals, and interaction moments. Additionally, I directed the visual experience of the website, including layout interaction logic and hover effects, to create an immersive and gamified digital space that reflected the fun and inclusive spirit of the Klankz community.
