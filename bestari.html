The first environment Pop-Culture event in Indonesia

Bestari Festival is a collaborative event between Pandemic Talks and Idea Fest that combines talkshows and workshops focused on sustainable and creative practices. It aims to provide a welcoming space for urban communities in the Jabodetabek area to gain inspiration, engage in discussions, and collaborate on environmental topics. Through its engaging Pop-Culture format, Bestari Festival serves as a gateway for individuals to reconnect with nature and embark on a journey towards a more sustainable lifestyle.
